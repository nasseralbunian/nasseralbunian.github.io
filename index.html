About

Who Am I?

I am currently a faculty member (Assistant Professor) and a trainer at the Institute of Public Administration in the Digital Transformation and Information Technology Department. I provide training for a wide range of organisations and individuals on topics related to information technology such as Information Security, Digital Transformation, topics related to Software Engineering, and Emerging Technologies (more specifically, Blockchain technology). I have already trained over 700+ employees from different sectors and delivered over 10+ training programs.

I hold a PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Sheffield where I was a member of the Testing group. My PhD research mainly focused on improving the automation of software test generation. I obtained a BSc in Computer Science from Qassim University and MSc in Computer Science from the University of New Brunswick .
