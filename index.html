About

Who Am I?

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Sheffield. My PhD research mainly focuses on studying search behavior in Search-Based Software Testing (SBST) under the supervision of Gordon Fraser and Dirk Sudholt. I hold a BSc in Computer Science from Qassim University and MSc in Computer Science from the University of New Brunswick .

My research interests lie primarily in the area of Software Engineering, in particular software quality, search-based software engineering, empirical software engineering, and evolutionary testing.
